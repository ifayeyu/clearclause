About ClearClause

A free tool built to help freelancers get paid, protect their work, and grow their business with confidence.

What Is ClearClause?

ClearClause is a free, browser-based contract generator built specifically for independent freelancers and contractors. It was created to solve a simple problem: most freelancers know they need a written contract, but professional legal templates are expensive, confusing, and slow to set up. ClearClause makes it possible to generate a professional, comprehensive freelance contract in under five minutes — for free, with no account required.

The tool supports six contract types covering the most common freelance service categories: general freelance work, web design and development, graphic design, copywriting, photography, and social media management. Each contract type includes the clauses most relevant to that kind of work, pre-filled with reasonable default terms that you can customize to your specific situation.

Our approach: ClearClause runs entirely in your browser. Your contract data — client names, project details, fees — is never sent to our servers and never stored. When you close the page, your data is gone. We generate your PDF locally on your device.

Why We Built ClearClause

Freelancers are the fastest-growing segment of the workforce, and the contract gap is real. Surveys consistently show that a large percentage of independent workers have experienced scope creep, disputes over deliverables, or late/non-payment — problems that a clear written agreement would have prevented or resolved. At the same time, hiring a lawyer to draft a custom contract for every new client isn't practical for most freelancers, especially those just starting out.

We built ClearClause to fill this gap. Professional, clear contracts should not be a privilege of large studios and established agencies. Every freelancer — whether they charge $500 or $50,000 per project — deserves a written agreement that protects them, sets clear expectations, and signals professionalism to their clients.

What ClearClause Generates

Every contract generated by ClearClause includes the essential elements of a professional service agreement:

  • Parties and effective date — clearly identifies the freelancer, client, and when the agreement takes effect
  • Scope of work — the specific services to be delivered, formatted for the contract type you've selected
  • Payment terms — total fee, deposit structure, payment schedule, accepted payment methods, and late payment policy
  • Revision policy — the number of included revision rounds and the rate for additional revisions
  • Timeline and deadlines — project start date, milestones, and final delivery date
  • Intellectual property — when and how ownership of the work transfers to the client
  • Termination clause — how either party can end the agreement and what happens financially
  • Limitation of liability and indemnification — mutual protection from third-party claims and liability caps
  • Signature block — space for both parties to sign and date the agreement

ClearClause Is Not a Law Firm

ClearClause is a technology tool, not a legal service. The contracts it generates are templates intended to cover the most common freelance scenarios, but they cannot account for every legal jurisdiction, industry-specific requirement, or unusual contractual arrangement. Laws vary significantly by state and country, and what is enforceable in one location may not be enforceable in another.

For straightforward freelance engagements at typical rates, our templates are a solid starting point that many freelancers use successfully. For high-value contracts, complex intellectual property arrangements, or situations with significant legal risk, we strongly recommend consulting a qualified attorney. Many attorneys offer contract review services at reasonable flat fees that are well worth the investment on larger projects.
